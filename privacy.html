Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 9, 2026

ScrollLock (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how ScrollLock collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile app and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • App Configuration Data: such as your selected blocked apps, sleep schedule, streak information, and app preferences.
  • Purchase Information: if you subscribe to ScrollLock Premium, Apple may provide us with limited subscription status information to verify access to premium features.
  • Device and App Usage Data: such as app version, device type, operating system version, and diagnostic information used to improve app performance.
  • Support Information: if you contact us, we may collect your email address and the contents of your message.

2. Screen Time and Family Controls

ScrollLock uses Apple Screen Time / Family Controls APIs to help block apps during your selected sleep schedule. This access is used only to provide the app’s core functionality.

We do not sell or share your Screen Time permissions or related data with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide and maintain ScrollLock’s features
  • Save your preferences and schedules
  • Display stats such as streaks, nights protected, and time reclaimed
  • Process subscriptions and restore purchases
  • Improve performance, reliability, and user experience
  • Respond to support requests

4. Data Storage

Some information may be stored locally on your device, including settings, schedules, selected apps, and progress data.

If ScrollLock uses cloud services in the future, this Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect what data is stored remotely and how it is protected.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited information only when necessary to:

  • Process subscriptions through Apple
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect the rights, safety, and integrity of ScrollLock and its users

6. Third-Party Services

ScrollLock may rely on third-party services such as Apple App Store, StoreKit, and related Apple frameworks for purchases, subscription management, and app distribution. These services are governed by their own privacy policies.

7. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide the app’s functionality, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Information stored locally on your device may remain until you delete the app or clear the app’s data.

8. Your Choices

  • You can stop using ScrollLock at any time
  • You can remove the app from your device
  • You can manage subscriptions through your Apple ID account settings
  • You can decline certain permissions, though some features may not function properly without them

9. Children’s Privacy

ScrollLock is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

10. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.
