Scroll Lock

Sleep Better. Scroll Less.

Scroll Lock helps users reduce late-night scrolling by automatically blocking distracting apps during sleep hours.

Users choose which apps are restricted, set their bedtime schedule, and Scroll Lock locks those apps until morning.

How it Works

• Choose the apps you want blocked
• Set your bedtime and wake time
• Apps automatically lock at night
• Everything unlocks in the morning
• Track streaks and nights protected

Why Scroll Lock

Most people lose hours every night to late-night scrolling. Scroll Lock helps you protect your sleep, reduce distractions, and build healthier screen habits.

Privacy

Scroll Lock uses Apple’s Screen Time / Family Controls framework to enforce user-selected app restrictions. The app does not collect personal browsing data or track user activity.
